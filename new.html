🚫 Anti-Ragging Committee & Policy

SJBIT has zero tolerance policy for ragging. The Institute views ragging as an uncivilized, inhuman practice. Ragging in all forms is totally banned inside and outside campus.

Objectives

  • Ragging free campus
  • Free grievance expression
  • Impartial solution
  • Healthy environment

Preventive Measures

Anti-Ragging Committee, Squad, Student Group, mentoring cell, awareness hoardings, online affidavits, 24/7 helpline.
